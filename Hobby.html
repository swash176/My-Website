Hobbies and Interests

Aviation * Fishing * Martial Arts * Driving Range

One of my greatest passions is aviation, I enjoy all aspects of the aviation world. I can be found plane spotting at random airports, or you can find me somewhere watching flight sim live streams, listening to aviation podcast, or if you look up and see a Cessna flying over that just may be me. I'm a collector of model airplanes, some would say I have more than enough Skymark models. What can I say it's aviation it’s my thing. You ever look and ask yourself, how does this machine soar through the air, like how is this possible?

If I'm not out doing something aviation related you may just find me on the shore or out on the water dropping a line, trying to catch some dinner. Fishing is an activity I got into through some martial arts buddies of mine, and I’ve enjoyed it ever since, one minute you can be sitting around waiting for a bite and the next you can be fighting a nice sized striped bass. Always good to catch up with friends and drop a line.

Martial Arts has been a part of my life since the age of 10, I’ve studied and gained ranking and teach in 3 different styles of martials arts: VeeJitsu, Goju Ryu and Shindo Ryu Karate. While always a student of the martial arts I do hold the distinction and honor of being an instructor.

Interestingly enough, I actually am not a fan of golf, I don’t watch any golf events and I couldn’t tell you what the rules to the game are, however I do have a lot of fun going out to TopGolf and trying to hit that ball as hard as possible and get it downrange. I like playing some of the mini games that TopGolf has, one of my favorites being the TopGolf edition Angry Birds. Going to the driving range is a great way to have fun and connect with friends and family.
